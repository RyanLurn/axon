1. What is the sum of all prime numbers less than 100?

2. A farmer has both chickens and rabbits. He counts 40 heads and 112 legs in total. How many chickens and how many rabbits does he have?

3. What is the 15th Fibonacci number? (Where F(1) = 1, F(2) = 1, F(3) = 2, ...)

4. A ball is dropped from a height of 120 meters. Each time it bounces, it rises to 65% of the height it fell from. What is the total distance traveled by the ball by the time it hits the ground for the 8th time? Round to 2 decimal places.

5. What is the remainder when 2^100 is divided by 13?
